How to Play

Movement:

  • W - Move Forward
  • S - Move BackWard
  • A / D - Turn Left / Right
  • Left Shift - Sprint (Increase Speed)
  • L - Switch to a Different Player

Attacking (When in Possession of the Ball):

  • J - Pass
  • K - Shoot

Tips:

  • Make sure you come in contact with the ball to ensure you have proper possession to use actions.
  • The Green Triangle turns Blue when in range to make a passing/shooting/clearing action with the ball.
  • The ball must be between you, the direction you are facing, and the Green Triangle must turn Blue.
